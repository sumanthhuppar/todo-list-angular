About Us

Welcome to our To-Do List App! We are a team of developers passionate about productivity and organization. Our goal is to provide you with a simple yet powerful tool to manage your tasks effectively.

With our app, you can create, edit, and delete tasks with ease. You can also organize your tasks into categories, set deadlines, and mark tasks as completed.
